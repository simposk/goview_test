“LOGIN 2017” is the largest tech and innovation festival in Baltic States. And this year we had the opportunity to be there and talk about virtual reality in schools. We did the hard work to understand what students and teachers want to see in our upcoming VR education platform. Connections between different type of people helps us to create a biggest VR developer community at schools.
